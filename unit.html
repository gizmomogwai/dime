    Module unit

    Print human readable units (e.g. time in days, hours, minutes or distance in km, m, cm, mm).

    Functions

    NameDescription
    mostSignificant(parts, nr) get the first nr of parts (or less if not enough parts are available).
    onlyRelevant(parts) get only relevant parts of an part array. relevant means all details starting from the first non 0 part.

    Structs

    NameDescription
    Unit A unit allows to easily print mixed resolution values. Typical examples include time (with hours, minutes, ...) and distances (with km, m, cm, mm). The unitclass simplifies definition of such things as well as transforming a high resolution value, to a supposedly more human readable representation. e.g. 3_610_123 would convert to 1h 0m 10s 123ms.
